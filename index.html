Odin Recipes

Hello! And welcome to my Odin Recipes HTML project. I have collected for you a three-course offering around the themes of latin heat, asian heat, and american heat.

This first recipe can be cut into quarters to present a delicious and refreshing appetizer. I'm talking about this Chicken Katsu Sandwich baby! These classic Louisianna flavors are given a crunchy twist with the addition of Japanese Panko crumbs.

Next is our main course! A hot and spicy Braised Eggplant Noodles The kick here comes from the addition of Korean hot pepper paste, Gochujang, which is also used in the preparation of kimchi. Watch out!

Finally, we're cooling off with a trip to Mexico. This tangy Mangonada is the perfect way to end our promenade around the culinary globe. Enjoy the contrast of cold and hot with this tasty frozen treat that can also be served as a smoothie!
